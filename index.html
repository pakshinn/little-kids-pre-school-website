Events

  1. 01.07.2023

    Nature Walk and Picnic

    This event is an opportunity for children to explore and learn about nature. The event will be held in a local park and children will have a chance to take a guided nature walk, learn about different plants and animals and enjoy a picnic lunch. The event is a great way to encourage children to appreciate nature and to get them out of the house and into the fresh air.

  2. 13.09.2023

    Arts and Crafts Extravaganza

    This event is a fun and creative way for children to learn and express themselves through art. The event will feature different stations with different art projects such as painting, drawing, and making crafts. All materials will be provided and the event will be held in a community center or school.

  3. 25.11.2023

    Science Experiment Day

    This event is an interactive and fun way for children to learn about science. The event will be held in a school or community center and will feature different stations with hands-on science experiments for children to try. Each station will have a different theme, such as chemistry, physics, or biology. The experiments will be safe and easy for children to perform.
